About Solar Incentive Finder

Solar Incentive Finder was built to solve a real problem: most homeowners don't know what solar incentives they qualify for. Federal programs, state rebates, utility incentives, and local grants are scattered across dozens of websites and government databases. We put them all in one place.

Our mission is to help every homeowner save money on solar energy by providing accurate, up-to-date information on incentives available in their specific zip code.

How Our Data Works

We compile incentive data from government sources including the Database of State Incentives for Renewables & Efficiency (DSIRE), the U.S. Department of Energy, and the National Renewable Energy Laboratory (NREL). Our data is updated regularly to reflect current programs.

Who We Are

We're a small team of solar advocates and homeowners passionate about making clean energy more accessible. We believe that going solar should be financially rewarding — and that starts with knowing what's available to you.

We are not affiliated with any solar installer, panel manufacturer, or energy company. Our incentive information is independent and unbiased.

Disclaimer

The information on this website is provided for general informational purposes only. While we make every effort to ensure accuracy, incentive programs can change without notice. We recommend verifying all information with the relevant agency or program administrator. This website does not provide financial, tax, or legal advice.
