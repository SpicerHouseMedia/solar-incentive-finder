Frequently Asked Questions

How much is the federal solar tax credit in 2026?

The federal solar Investment Tax Credit (ITC) is 30% of your total installation costs, including equipment, labor, and permitting. This credit has no maximum limit.

How do I claim the solar tax credit?

File IRS Form 5695 with your annual tax return. Your solar installer will provide documentation of the total system cost.

Are solar incentives available in my state?

Most states offer at least one solar incentive program. Enter your zip code above to find all programs available in your area.
