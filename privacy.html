Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Solar Incentive Finder ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

Zip Code: When you use our incentive finder tool, you voluntarily enter your zip code. This information is used solely to determine which solar incentives and rebate programs are available in your geographic area. Zip code data is not stored in association with personally identifiable information.

Usage Data: We use Google Analytics to collect information about how visitors interact with our website. This includes your browser type, device type, pages visited, time spent on pages, and approximate location based on your IP address.

How We Use Information

  • To display relevant solar incentive information based on your zip code
  • To analyze website traffic and improve our content
  • To understand which states and incentives are most searched for

Third-Party Services

Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand visitor behavior. Google may use the data it collects to personalize ads in its advertising network. Learn more at Google's Privacy Policy.

Affiliate Links: Some links on our website may be affiliate links through programs like Amazon Associates. When you click these links and make a purchase, we may receive a commission at no additional cost to you. These third-party websites have their own privacy policies.

Advertising

We may display advertisements on our website through Google AdSense and other advertising networks. These third-party ad networks use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to serve you relevant ads based on your browsing habits across the internet.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings or by visiting Network Advertising Initiative's opt-out page.

Cookies

We use cookies through Google Analytics to recognize repeat visitors and analyze usage patterns. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the analytics functionality but will not impact the core incentive lookup tool.

Data Security

We do not store zip codes in association with personal identifiers such as email addresses or IP addresses. Our website uses HTTPS encryption to protect data in transit.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us
