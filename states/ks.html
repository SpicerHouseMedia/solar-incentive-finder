Federal Incentives (Available in CA)

  • ✓
    Federal Solar Tax Credit (ITC)

    Claim 30% of your total solar installation cost as a credit on your federal taxes. No maximum limit. Available through 2032.

State & Local Programs

  • ✓
    Solar Property Tax Exclusion

    Solar installations are excluded from property tax assessments. Your home won't be taxed at a higher value because of your solar system.

  • ✓
    Net Energy Metering (NEM 3.0)

    California utilities must credit you for excess solar power sent to the grid. Under NEM 3.0, export rates are lower but you can still offset your bill significantly.

  • ✓
    SGIP Battery Rebates

    The Self-Generation Incentive Program offers rebates up to $1,000/kWh for home battery storage. Priority goes to high-fire-threat areas and medical baseline customers.
